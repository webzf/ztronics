# Recommended Hardware

These are the boards, displays and sensors used throughout Embedded Nerd tutorials.

## ESP32 Boards

* ESP32 DevKit
* ESP32-S3

## Sensors

* MPU6050
* DHT22
* HC-SR04

## Displays

* SSD1306 OLED
* I2C LCD Display

## Starter Kits

* ESP32 Starter Kit
* Arduino Starter Kit
